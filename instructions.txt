      Brick breaker Game: INSTRUCTIONS
Buttons:
1. P: pause the game    2. M: Main Menu
3. E: Exit the game

Levels:
Bricks have different strength according to
their respective colours.
1. Violet brick breaks by only one hit.
2. Blue brick breaks by two hits.
3. Green brick breaks by three hits.
4. Yellow brick breaks by four hits.
5. Orange brick breaks by five hit.
6. Red brick breaks by six hit.
